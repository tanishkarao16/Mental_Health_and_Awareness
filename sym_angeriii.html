UNDESTAND SYMPTOMS OF ANGER

  • To worry about small things.

    • It may so happen that you get angry frequently,but you are not sure about the reason that caused your anger. You may also find that you are losing control.

  • Inability to calm down.

    • It is mostly found that people with anger issues find it difficult to calm down. They might take a longer time to get rid of their anger.

  • Negative thinking.

    • If you get negative thoughts such as harming a person who was the reason for your anger, or feel negatively about everything around you, you have chances of heavily anger issues.

  • Communication Problems.

    • Anger can often lead to a situation where you cannot communicate with others. Anger can cause a emotional stress on you and thepeople around you, which can lead to miscommunication.

  • Not being able to be mindful.

    • Being mindful means to be completely aware of what is happening in and around you surroundingly when you are angry. If you are not able to judge/ understand what is going on during a situation, you probably have an anger management issue.

  • Finding it difficult to release.

    • If you are finding it difficult to relax after you are angry, or feel very stressed, or fixed it difficult to focus in your life/work, you are in a high riseof facing anger management issues.

  • Rumination

    • Rumination is the situation when you constantly think about the reason that made you angry, which might lead to worsening your anger. You expresses emotional and psychological stress.
